Projects

Designly.com

During YC S13 Nate, Steve, Pat, Jordan, and I built Designly.com: a WYSIWYG website builder that mirrored the functionality of Adobe InDesign. It allowed users to drag and drop content anywhere they wanted on the page (for reals), as well as taking care of hosting.

One Second on the Internet

Steve and I put our heads together to try and bring traffic to the Designly site for our launch. We came up with One Second on the Internet: a reflection on how far we've come in the past 20 years. It drove about 1.1M uniques, 9k signups, and garnered a like from Mark Zuckerberg on Facebook.

American Eagle Social Media

Worked with a team of students from Katz to advise American Eagle Outfitters on social media strategy for an upcoming international expansion. Research involved competitive analysis of the current market, in-depth analysis of current communication and promotional strategies, and consumer behavior analysis for target demographics.

Giant Eagle Analytics

Looked at 3 years (3 gigs) worth of sales data for a specific product category. Analyzed yearly trends down to the brand level for 5 main subcategories, researched relevant consumer behavior patterns via Nielsen's SPECTRA, and proposed a number of marketing initiatives to Giant Eagle.
